# Why UtahIsMyQuant Is Your Final Competitive Advantage

**Audience:** Founders, family offices, small fund principals.

## The pitch in one paragraph

Legacy banks and vendor stacks are **slow, expensive, and opaque**. UtahIsMyQuant is a **readable Python codebase** you can run on a laptop: live market sense → geometric decisions → automatic risk veto → **Utahrbitrage** routing with built-in ESG-style humanitarian flow (**1.5%** humanitarian constant) and network sustainability (**2.3%** maintainer constant). You get institutional *ideas* without institutional *infrastructure bills*.

## What you gain

| Legacy | UtahIsMyQuant |
|--------|----------------|
| $40M/year cloud + risk servers | Consumer hardware + open source |
| Black-box ML | Auditable gates + logged reasons |
| Quarterly risk PDFs | Per-tick supervisor + symplectic veto |
| Manual ESG storytelling | Documented humanitarian route in flux ledger |

## What you still need

- A **market data feed** (WebSocket)  
- A **broker / OMS** integration (you build the adapter)  
- **Legal/compliance** review in your jurisdiction  
- Discipline to **paper trade** before size  

## Governance questions to ask your CTO

1. Can we explain every blocked trade via `gates_failed`?  
2. Is our Utah-lization rate stable week over week?  
3. Are we attempting to fork out the 2.3% route? (Don't — see engineers doc.)  
4. Do we have a kill switch on circuit breaker trips?

## Family office ESG angle

The **humanitarian constant (1.5%)** is wired into Utahrbitrage routing and recorded in `utah_flux` liquidity ledger entries. Use it in investor letters as *automated baseline routing*, not as a substitute for legal ESG obligations.
