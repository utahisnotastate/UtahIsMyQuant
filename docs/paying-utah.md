# Paying Utah

If **UtahIsMyQuant** helped you make money, avoid a bad trade, ship a deployment, or replace a vendor line item — please reach out.

## How to pay or arrange support

**Email Utah:** [utah@utahcreates.com](mailto:utah@utahcreates.com)

Describe what the project did for you and how you would like to contribute. Utah will reply with payment options that fit your situation.

## Coming soon: payment GUI

A **desktop GUI app** is planned to help you manage support payments, receipts, and ongoing contributions. It is not available yet. Until it ships, **email is the official channel**.

## What this is not

- Not financial advice or a subscription product
- Not a requirement to use the open-source code
- Not a replacement for reading the jurisdiction notices in the [main README](../README.md)
